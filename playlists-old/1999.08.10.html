Fun last show. Wonderful to be able to play vinyl on the single turntable and I made good use of it. This show was a work of one tape deck, two CD players and a turntable. Set moved from a disjointed beginning towards minimal techno then over towards melody based peices and finally into more abstract downtempo and drill n bass but more beat oriented work. A good ending to the summer for me.
(this was a monday night)
