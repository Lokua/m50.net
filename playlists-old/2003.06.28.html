These sets went rather smoothly I think. Thanks to Praxis, as well as all of Kompute for their support on this one. The first set I just wanted to cover some energetic material, then did a focus on Christian Morgenstern who sadly passed away this week. I preceeded that focus with a pair of tracks by Detroit's Drexciya (albeit both tracks under different aliases), a duo who also recently suffered a loss (James Stinson, last year) and left behind a trail of influential music and expression. Morgenstern was a huge contributor to Kanzleramt records, and founded Forte, supporting others like Matt Mercer, Codex & Flexor and Dr Shingo through this imprint. This was a talented artist who's influences, tracks, and efforts will be felt far into the future.
