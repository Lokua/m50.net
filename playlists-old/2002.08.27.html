etc 2002.Aug.27 21:30 90 minutes @ WNUR, Evanston-Chicago

Along for this set were Gabriel Palomo and Tim Xavier in support of their new 773 event, an all-techno free weekly. Tim sat this one out but Gabriel layed down a deep thick hard techno groove, starting out with more abstract numbers along the lines of Monolake and 8-bit-construction set and ended up closing with stompers, playing more than a couple Xavier cuts along the way.
