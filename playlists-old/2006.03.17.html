updated 2006.04.28: what an incredible night. the thing that's etched in my memory from it was dancing right in front of the booth, facing the back of the room with niko, who I hardly ever see anymore, and tzusing framed perfectly behind her, all blue light and perfect music and just being lost in the moment and feeling it like a laser through me at the same time. and partying with emily from beloit, who was totally out of her mind and a ton of fun. I remember Geoffery from Consumers coming just after I put on the Signaldrift record and standing in the booth, cheering me on for a good chunk of the set - actually until I put on the Mark Broom v Autechre and told him to listen to it in the main room. Penny and Francesca and Maya (and Raoul I think? not sure if I saw him) came in earlier than that and Francesca came into the booth for a moment, Maya after that and Lenny the manager got us all redheaded sluts.
