Ubiquity
Total booster of an evening, cerrtainly one of the more reaffirming of recent memory. Not only did I see Derek (LMS), Geoffery (CRD), Josh (ST) and Liza there, and not only were TRS 80 awesome and the turnout grand, but I also met Jake Elliot of Musk.exe, Lance /asdf, Matt Taylor from NU, and some girl from Mixer. Fun of course to get new Dan the Automator & Gabriel Palomo wax, but more fun was several people coming up to me through the course of the evening who were already aware of my radio show but didn't know me in person, nice to make those links between presence in different media and especially encouraging to know that people are getting reached! TRS 80 had a great set, some fresh new material in there as well as reworks of some older stuff (like 5000 Pounds of Thrust remixed!) and their synced-video presentation was a great compliment to the evening. I knew they were going to rock it, so I'm glad that many people came out to support and got treated to a memorable show. People were even dancing! At Danny's!
