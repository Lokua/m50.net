I was very happy with this as a concluding group of sets for a good weekend (Thursday included) of music. Joel gave props around the Troubleshooter section, which is probably as weird as I got all night, Kim Alpert (Binary Mine) kept requesting me to play more records befor that set so it was good that I got to use the rest of my record bag then. Dined on the finest extra fries and the last piece of pizza. On the way out, met a guy who said he needed a ride to Naiperville or something, but I guess he was just a drunk because I waited around for 10 minutes while he kept asking other folks for money and then finally he declined my offer. Just as well. Recording seems to have turned out well too, will post soon. Cheers to Zelle for coming out to spread the word about the new compilation, cheers to Slava for coming out to toss me the MD, and cheers to the guitarist from Timeout Drawer who came out to say hi. Happy birthday Joel.
