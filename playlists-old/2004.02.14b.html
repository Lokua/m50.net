that was a fun time. definitely sorry that i didn't record the set (due to quick setup time) because I was 'on' for the whole set, from mixing out of quantazelle's last track to closing with zoetrope. apparently the antics of glitchtronics & hardcore throughout the evening leading up to my set, coupled with the valentine's day homebodies and the big out-of-town lineup next week contributed to a less-packed dancefloor than the house night the week prior. oh well, I'm glad that I got to play again, dedicated "pontape" to derrick from lms, saw liza & liz, j3s & atomly, vijay, gabe, shift, and marc martin as well as nathan ballard (who I'm not sure if came inside, I wasn't exactly playing techno though so you can hardly blame him) there, met jeff stoner and a guy (matty?)from betty's blue star. pretty nice to play a set in such a supportive atmosphere, with people dancing, and only 1 request (and for matthew dear instead of hey ya!).
