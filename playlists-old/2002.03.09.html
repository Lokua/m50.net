Marko Katic and Josh Werner of Gramaphone Records, Antenna International, and Material (was http://www.material.hm/ ) supplied some choice beats this week. Incidentally, Josh and Rocket (the wonderdog) tagged along unexpectedly and stuck it out for the whole set. Mike and JJ made it out for Clinically Inclined and Fen came for most of etc. A night of future and past colliding both on Etc and Clinically Inclined. CiM show has been announced, see Covalent or etc-news for details. Adds this week from Kfever99, zlft550, and david46208. Josh started out the set with some recent prospects for the label, and from there we broke into the kinky tech-house stuff, dipping into minimalism and retro-house and -electro along the way. Rather liked this set.

Next week could very well be my final show...
