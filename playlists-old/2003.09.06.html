Some nice gear here by nickd, huge thanks to him for coming down on 12 hours notice. I think our set on Saturday night should be pretty hot as always. His Canadian friend - Ian I believe - sat in on his set and seemed to be getting into the vibe. I'll link to his blog or whatever here as soon as I get to that part in the set or when someone e-mails me with it. My set wasn't as well formed as it could potentially have been but I think I covered a pretty big timespan as compared with usual, jumping back and forth between brand-spanking-new or pre-release material and older cuts (Conceiled Project, Beltram, Akufen's first release and so on...) All this was a replacement set for Proswell and Reverend Robert Sinewave, who will both be coming in later on this Fall.
