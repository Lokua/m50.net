This set went really well, I was very happy with my first experience tag-teaming with Tobias, and my first time visiting (and playing at) the Funky Buddha. Props to Tobias for setting this up on such short notice, to the small but exuberant crowd, and to Dave Siska for coming through. Props also to David, subk, Aaron, Nick, & Matt for material. I'm sad there was no recording because this set fit together very well. This was also the first time I met Milena.
