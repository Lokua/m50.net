That was a lot of calls. Thank you to: and I don't remember what order everyone called in but this should be everyone, and they were almost all adds: Sunny (congratulations on the tickets), Julio (same) Mike (same), Zachary, Eugene (after a long time!) and Dené (congratulations on the tickets). A solid show I think, this was intended as a prep for the Smartbar event I just got invited to. I spun for about 30 minutes longer than I expected and kept a decent handle on things the whole time I think. Passing through the studio were Jeff&Liz, Dave the PD, Alex and another apprentice, and then finally I was followed by David Kullen formerly known as Dave The Slave. Wish I'd recorded it, but if I had I guess it wouldn't have been as good. My show diaries are really getting out of hand. I wonder if anyone reads them. Goodnight.
