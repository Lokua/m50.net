Dakini is a label out of Japan run by Gio & Keiko aka Makyo. With 6 stunning discs out since the Winter of '98, Dakini is well on it's way to becoming a crucial source of techno-fusion music blending many exotic influences into such things as Balinese space dub, Zen dub, and Shanti-sonics. Offering the work of more traditional native instrumentalists, urban chill DJs, and female vocalists, Dakini provides top-notch vibrations! This episode will showcase many releases from Dakini.

Dakini Discography:
Makyo- Shringara [DAK9901], Vismaya [DAK9902], + Devadasi remixes [DAK9900]
Padmasana (Makyo + D. Hikari) [DAK9903]
Ochi Brothers (Yoshiaki & Yoshihisa Ochi) [DAK9904]
Sky Dancing (new compilation): featuring exclusive material from Makyo, Jaia, Padmasana, Drift (Adham Shaikh + Tim Floyd), Professor Trance, and others

Look for a new Padmasana cd-single called "A Universal Consciousness" which will be out in July. The next full-length from Dakini will be a trance album by Jaia in August, and also the new Makyo album "Yakshini" will be out in Sept/Oct and should get a US release as well.-pHonaut
