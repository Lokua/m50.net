recorded... went well.
this was the most amazing night of the tour, and may well have outdone my night with ellen allien earlier this year. the buildup over the course of the evening was palpable, holocene turned into a sweatbox, and it was great to see even strategy getting down like a madman. the pair of latex fetishists were great (not to mention the fact that even THEY were dancing), and the only reason the party ended was that the venue opted to kick everyone out around 3am, by which point the crowd had diminished somewhat but was still every bit as enthusiastic on the dancefloor. this event got coverage all over the mercury, senor frio did a great job with the lineup, there were oddball microbe projections on some kind of 8mm projector for the middle third of the show, and I think everyone's sets went well.
