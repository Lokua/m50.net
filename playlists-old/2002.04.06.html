This week's set featured the talents of Josh Werner, Mani Miglani, and Marko Katic. Also in the studio were Cathryn, Monika and Carrie. Recieved a phone call (add) from James as well. This got mostly recorded, although my part is on a tape that is starting to get a little worn out. Too many set recordings. Does someone want to donate a bunch of hi-fi audio tapes to record sets on with newer oxides for brighter sounds? I did an Itiswhatitis spotlight this evening that was tech-housey, featured a new track by Lackluster on Merck Records an old track by Repeats (Plaid & Mark Broom = FIND!) and a track by Surgery's own Tim Koch. A quiet night that went well also I think. "Props" go out to all the people who came down and links to the organizations they create and support. Gramaphone Records, Antenna International, OBS Productions, Material.
