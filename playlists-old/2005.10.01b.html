Aki's mix...
no setlist

This was one of the most beautiful evenings I have ever played at. It was an honour to be a part of and I don't think I'll ever forget the ending of the 3 tracks that Aki played for Hiro, and then our drawn-out goodbyes at sunrise. Finally, driving up Lasalle with Hiro before we split paths. I hope that someday I have a going away party this good. Hiroshi will be dearly missed and those who receive him in Hiroshima are lucky to have him. Thanks to everyone involved, especially to the 1087 crew (Tamas, Nick), Captain Irresponsible, and to Aki (for providing the mix tape and so much encouragement and support) and Gina (for her short attention span and excitement about music). And thank you to Hiro, of course, for inviting me to be a part of it. It was nice to see Beth there again, see lots of Hiro's friends from the Art Institute, and Burton L from illmeasures. As I write this, the sound of an ice cream truck coming up the street behind me seems like a cue to finish up.
