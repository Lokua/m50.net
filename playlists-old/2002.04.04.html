Set generously provided by Chris Widman and LSE of Smartbar's Abstract Sessions and WLUW's Abstract Science. I thought this evening went rather well, certainly smoothly and a nice CD only selection of music (other than the plaid 7" earlier on). A more laid back Ambient techno feel like back in the day. Foo on all the prank callers. Foo also on overzelous (read: male) admirers. Horray for WARP, Variety is the Spice of Life, Pitchcadet, Cranky, Invisible, Eat This and Bip-hop for providing the excellent material for this evening.This set was not recorded. :
