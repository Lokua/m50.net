the rules:
"Music selections are limited to industrial music and its many sub-genres, including (but not limited to) industrial, electro, EBM, futurepop, synthpop, darkwave, dark ambient, IDM, glitch, electroclash, noise, rhythmic noise, powernoise, and experimental.

I am not interested in goth, 80s, techno, trance, drum 'n bass, jungle, hip hop, breakbeat, rock, heavy / speed / thrash / death metal, alternative, rap, oldies, classical, punk, a capella, country, western OR any other type of music."

so... the set looks something like:
7 ambient
8 noise
9 loud glitch
10 medium glitch
11 soft glitch
12 electro
1 synthpop

Well, that was the plan. Not so many dancing people so I ended up staying in the glitchy stuff till the end. Overall, pretty happy with the set and a satisfactory turnout. Shouts to Joe, Salma, Serge, Nial, Miles, Adam, Matt & Liz.
