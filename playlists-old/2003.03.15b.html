Really interesting gig, loving the black lighting and the setup was certainly... unique. I could hear what was going on but there was some cool (non-painful) feedback effects going on that once I got the hang of made it a bit of a more flexible sound. Strangely certain records didn't want to play properly (Wu Tang?). I even tried bowling but I only hit the gutter so I gave up. Oh, and I met Protman & Quantazelle. Thanks to Emily C & Holly for tagging along & Mike & Tracy & Diana coming out later on. Feminaexmachina gets mad props for not only hooking up the gig but also picking up her own turntable at the last minute for it and being the friendliest person there! Wow. Oh, and I kind of played hiphop?
