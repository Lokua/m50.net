Successful set executed by the one and only, Matthew Ross Davis. Using homemade piezo mikes, contact microphones, and a wide assortment of live instrumentation ranging from a spring-powered portable echo chamber to a glass sprite bottle to a (newly acquired) cactus to the tin to rocks and miniature cymbals. This was truly a performance to behold and translated well to the airwaves. Always fresh and unique, he played material from upcoming releases as well as what sounded like a live interpretation of String Theory II off of his Trolling for Olives ep on Metatron Press. After that we were treated to an obviously improptu q & a session where I got to hear about upcoming Craque appearances and new Consumer Research and Development Label goodies also to feature etc friends String Theory. I opened and closed the set with a little minimal dubbed out bassy ambient assortment and threw in some tracks off of the essential recent Isan reissue compilation on Morr music.
