A bit uneven show at the beginning because of my ANNOYING TICKETING by UNIVERSITY POLICE. I had to go and move my car too, I guess they were going to tow me or something. So somehow I managed to do that and then get back into the mix but the first couple minutes were... of uneven intensity. After that I think I settled in to my usual style of interspersed hip-hop and abstraction. This whole thing was following a tight 3 hour set by Charles Adler's Subsystence (IE only compatible) program and I ended up playing until 4am. Around then I got a call telling me I was "fucking up the song" with a hangup and I was planning on going home anyway, so I did. That CD mixer is fierce. I'm kid606 now.
