Whoa, that felt like a long set. Rather interesting textures at points, certainly one of my more ambitious to date. In a large part this ambition was due to Tom's gung-ho no-holds-barred style so it was good to interact with that. Phonaut played a solid set of course too this evening and was very patient with my sheer exhaustion. Thanks to Mike for messaging during the show. Not many calls and no list adds. Guess one must have "off" weeks sometimes though. At least people are still visiting the website. Playlist gets complicated when you're playing from 4-5 sources at once. Can't be helped. By the way, thanks to Jaret for taking the time to note most of DJdMAN's playlist.
