First show back. Went rather splendidly, marked as the first show since the introduction of digital archiving last week, and the first show which will havea mix released concurrently with its playlist. Much new material introduced: Pilote, The Wormholes, Kid-606, Heil. Shorter shows will mean more commentary I suspect. Odd note: Before I got to the studio it was full of LoTekers, after I left it was full of freeformers. During my show it was just me.
