DJ Skinny: "You're such a chameleon. When you're filling in for a downtempo DJ, you spin like you're Peter Kruder; the next night, you're John Acquaviva for a techno show. It's impressive." The first half an hour of this set was dub and dub techno because, well, I wanted to play that. The second half, however, had a more interesting theme, namely: a radio and internet mix show that I've been following for some years, Variety Is The Spice of Life on WMUC-FM College Park, MD. http://www.varietyisthespice.com/ host Christine Moritz has been offered a residency at Eighteenth Street Lounge (http://www.eslmusic.com/) in DC this weekend, so with this week marking her first regular appearance I thought it'd be appropriate to do a set in the theme of her signature style. Upon hearing the set, she described it as a "Minority Report version" of her show so I think I can be happy with the somewhat less funky and fun impersonation I did. Check out her show on Monday nights for the real thing, though.
Negative Space was Emir's show.
