Magic Silver Box and I. What can I say about this set? Well, for one, have you ever heard psytrance and hard techno played side by side so well? Even slipped in some breaks for good measure. This kid let me play on his weekly show at KCSB, official station of University of California, Santa Barabara. Their campus is nutty at night. We had a lot of fun with it. By the way, congratulations to Steve and Olivia on their wonderful marriage (the other reason I was out in California this time). The archive for this set should be available in time, assuming that the CD somehow gets to me before it is attacked by toothpaste or some other such substance while still in the possession of the Magic Silver one. We were joined in the studio by noneother than Dr Snax. Lucky us.
