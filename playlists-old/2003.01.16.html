Abstract Science 2003.01.16 240minutes featuring m50
on WLUW 88.7 FM

Crazy set due to technical difficulties: the two turntables had isolated channel outputs, so that one came out of each speaker with no mixture of the signals. I was in high spirits nonetheless for some reason. Got a call from technopixel and messages from dnstodd and dj xanax. Thanks to LSE and Chris Widman for setting this up - even though they didn't promote it at all on any of their lists. OH WELL.
