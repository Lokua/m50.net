tuesday night at Echo: metromix writes: "Located in the former Yvette space, Dragonfly offers upscale Sichuan, Hunan and Mandarin cuisine served in a swanky lounge setting. The Asian-inspired decor features red velvet booths and gold accents. At night, the dining room walls open up to accommodate well turned-out barhoppers." I'll be playing until midnight, expect chilled out hiphop instrumentals and ambient techno. I played here last week and recommend the food and the ambience.
