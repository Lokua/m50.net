nickd birthday set...
http://dj.nickd.org/ for details

I had a ton of fun with this dark, chunky, gross little set. The best part was when they cut all the lights and there was just the thumping sound of german swing techno screaming out of the little speakers in the tiny basement space. Made me want to play for longer. Xanax's parents were there and NickD took over in time to play as his birthday rolled over. I think Deluge and Joe Z played after that but I was long gone to my radio show. Lots of people I knew went so that was super cool too... Rock, Coen, & JJ were all there from the plex, Sarah from rock show and 3s from hiphop were there, Greg from philosophy was there, Alanna from Niteskool was there. Even Skinny made an appearance well before the show began. Who else was there?
