This set also marked the christening of our collaboration as "Fucking Ass Record Ninjas," originally a term used to denote the gremlins who make off with your record when you put it in the wrong sleeve but from now on also synonymous with the breakbeat-acid progressive statium-house ragga dark-techelectro stylings of m50 v nickd.
