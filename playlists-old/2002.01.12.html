Quick comments: Lots of stuff happening on this, the first show of 2002. Olivia, Sir K, Alicia in the studio beforehand, crazy rock guys recording in the other studio until the wee hours, tag teaming with W0rmgear and dodging around him and Fen. Calls from Sergey, Serge, Linelinc, and an IM & list add from Radditz273 rounded out a late starting, late finishing evening. Set turned out alright. Managed to avoid several technical pitfalls (needles in particular managed to sound half decent in spite of not tracking at all on some records I queued) and remain consistant for almost the entire set. Navigated a rather fluctuating level of sonic intensity. All this followed a best of 2001 show by DPS.
