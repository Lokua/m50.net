Well, I've been slacking in the etc-web as livejournal department. I'll try and catch up a bit here. Fun dance music set tonight, made it from progressive house and broken beat to perlon sound to minimal tech to electro and detroit. Logical progression. Fair number of phone calls, gave away tickets for Kid Koala and Vladislav Delay shows this week (now don't you wish you were listening?). Big thanks to Emily for recording this show for me, came off wonderfully. Was in competition with the big BT show at Norris University Center tonight as well as the Dimbiman & Peter Ford show at Redno, but people listened anyway. The show kicked off, in fact, with a cryptic phone call over the air from those two + Mani en route from the airport or somewhere. A couple dedication tracks, My Friend going out to xine881 of Variety Is The Spice Of Life for her help with the show, and Sydney Harbour Bridge going out to the little astronaut. Wasn't really on the web so didn't get any IMs but got calls from Vadim, Dave P, Gustavo and Dave Siska (all adds).
