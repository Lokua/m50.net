Thanks to Lance, Chuck, JJ, Mae, Nicci, My Crotch, Noomenon, and Ellie for all listening and calling in. Proswell played a very interesting, diverse and abrupt live set of reworks of past tunes and fresh new ones, some apparently made day of performance. His performance was a great arguement for a minimal set-up. I played a set after his. It was a bit of a mess of an evening for me, inspite of all the positive feedback. I was happy with the ambient beginning but was too sick and too exhausted to really get into the harder minimal set I tried to play later on. After listening to the archive though, it doesn't seem to show through that much so I'm happy at least with the results if not the process. Listen and decide for yourself.
